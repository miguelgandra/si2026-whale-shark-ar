👤 About Me
Miguel Gandra, PhD Marine Conservation Scientist · CCMAR & IMAR m3gandra@gmail.com

I am a Marine Biologist at CCMAR and IMAR (Portugal), having recently completed my PhD (Summa Cum Laude) in January 2026. As a PI funded by multiple NGOs (including the Save Our Seas and FAM Foundations), I lead and collaborate on national and international projects to advance marine megafauna conservation.

My work sits at the intersection of high-resolution biologging, computational biology, and big data. I am also dedicated to innovating Digital SciComm through novel tools and open-source development, transforming complex telemetry data into impactful stories for ocean protection.
